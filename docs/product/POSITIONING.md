# Castor Positioning

## What Castor is
Castor is a creator intelligence platform for podcast operators, combining feed ingestion, sponsorship attribution, performance diagnostics, and AI-assisted content insight.

## Who it is for
- Independent and network podcast creators
- Agencies managing multi-show portfolios
- Revenue and growth teams optimizing sponsorship outcomes

## Why it matters
Podcast teams struggle to connect content, audience behavior, and revenue impact. Castor provides one system to monitor performance and improve monetization decisions.

## Why Castor wins
- Deep vertical focus on podcast data pipelines and attribution
- Compounding data moat from content + engagement + campaign outcomes
- Operational maturity (telemetry, compliance, reporting) suitable for scaling creators and agencies
